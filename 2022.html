S.No. Status Solution
1 Pending at secretariat for Aadhar Authentication Student need to visit any Village/Ward Secretariat to undergo biometric Authentication. Student need to verify the displayed mother bank account details are valid or not before biometric authentication.If any details are found wrong they can update in Navasakam logins of WEA/WES in the native sachivalayam The sachivalayam tagged to the household of the student can be known by entering the adhar number in the link given below. https://gramawardsachivalayam.ap.gov.in/GSWS/#!/knowYourVolunteer
2 Pending at College for Aadhar Authentication Students should give their bio-metric authentication at college immediately. Student should contact scholarship staff / Principal of the college and complete it.. Otherwise their application will be treated as ineligible.
3 Pending at College for Principal esign Student should contact scholarship staff / Principal of the college to forward his/her application to District Welfare Office
4 OTA completed at District Officer This status means the application is in "Ready for release" stage and will be released in next phase of releases, subject to eligibility displayed in six step validation or secretariat verification.
5 Previous year discontinued If the scholarship amount released during previous discontinued course is paid back to government, then the application will be enabled for processing.
6 Previous year detained Student Availed scholarship for a course and again applied for the same course and course year in succeeding year this status will be displayed. Student is not eligible to avail scholar for applied year as he already availed in previous year. He can apply for next year.
7 Change of Course name or Course year Student need to raise the Grievance through his login or College login by uploading a copy of letter taken from College Principal and allotment order
8 Pending for Verification at Secretariat The student has to approach the native village/ward secretariat and undergo the verification in the login of Nava sakam. If found eligible, amount will be released in next schedule. The sachivalayam tagged to the household of the student can be known by entering the adhar number in the link given below. https://gramawardsachivalayam.ap.gov.in/GSWS/#!/knowYourVolunteer
9 CET Status NO The student has to upload CET allotment order either in his/her login or through college login. It will be verified by district officer and then will be eligible for sanction in next phase.
10 College is not empanelled for the JVD scheme with Higher Education Department Once the college gives consent to get empanelled in the JVD scheme, then the student will be eligible for release of JVD schemes.
11 Family of student is not eligible under six income eligibility conditions As per the data base of government, family is not eligible for JVD scheme, for the reason mentioned in the status of application. Opportunity was already given to students to file objections on it as part of social audit as per schedule notified, in case the reason is not valid. If objection is not filed within the scheduled time, then application is confirmed as ineligible..
12 Pending for release for PG students of private colleges The amounts will be released in due course for the students in private PG colleges.
If the student applied for the National level Scholarship Schemes, he has to choose to get benefit from only one source/scheme and return the other benefit, if sanctions are received from both the schemes. Otherwise, it will amount to misuse of the government money and will be subject to other disciplinary actions.
